MY.EARTH is a community and environmental app. It creates a visualization of pollution levels changing over time. It aims at raising awareness regarding pollution issues around the globe, both locally and internationaly.

It shows how space data can help on earth, looking at pollution levels from a global point of view. It gathers satellite pictures and environmental datas from NASA, elected organisations internationally, as well as city admninistrators, national organisation and individual data collectors locally. The visualization enhances pollution issues and improvement over time as it spans from 1970 to nowadays. Beginners and specialists can use this app to get accurate data or as an educational tool on environmental matters.
